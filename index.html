Crusted Chicken

Crusted chicken is a tasty and versatile dish that can be prepared in many ways. The crust can be made from a variety of ingredients, such as breadcrumbs, crushed nuts, or even cheese, and it adds a nice texture and flavor to the chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (you can use plain or seasoned)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or melted butter

Steps to make the dish:

  1. In one shallow dish, place the flour.
  2. In a second dish, whisk together the eggs and milk.
  3. In a third dish, combine the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, shaking off excess.
  5. Dip the floured chicken into the egg mixture, allowing any excess to drip off.
  6. Coat the chicken with the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  7. Heat olive oil or melted butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  8. Add the breaded chicken breasts and cook for about 4-6 minutes per side, or until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature should reach 165째F or 74째C).

  10. Place the breaded chicken breasts on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or lightly greased.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the crust is crispy.
